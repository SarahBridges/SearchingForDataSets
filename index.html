Unemployment rates and earnings by educational attainment, 2018

DataSet

This dataset contains data for two relationship: the relationship between education-level and unemployment (by percentage) of that education-level for persons aged 25 or older; the relationship between education-level and the median of weekly earnings for that education-level for persons aged 25 or older. From this dataset, I can compare how education correlates with employeement as well as how education-level correlates with payrate.
